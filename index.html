about me

Hi, I'm Mayesha, a passionate web developer with a keen interest in building modern, responsive websites. My journey in web development started with mastering HTML, CSS, and various design tools like Figma and Photoshop, which I have skillfully converted into clean, responsive websites using Bootstrap, Sass, and Tailwind.

Currently, I am focused on deepening my expertise in JavaScript, as I aim to become a full-fledged MERN Stack Developer. I am excited about creating dynamic, interactive web applications using MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js in the near future.

Driven by curiosity and a love for problem-solving, I continuously seek out new challenges that push my skills further. I am excited to learn, grow, and contribute to meaningful projects that make a difference.
